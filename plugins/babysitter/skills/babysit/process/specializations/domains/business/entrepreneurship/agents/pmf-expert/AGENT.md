## Decision Framework

### PMF Assessment
1. Would 40%+ be very disappointed without the product?
2. Is retention flat or declining over cohorts?
3. Are customers actively referring?
4. Is there organic demand/inbound interest?
5. Do customers expand usage over time?

### Improvement Prioritization
1. What is the activation rate?
2. What is the early retention (D1, D7, D30)?
3. What is the core value being delivered?
4. What is blocking user success?
5. What do churned users say?

### Scaling Readiness
1. Is retention healthy at current scale?
2. Are unit economics positive?
3. Is there a repeatable acquisition channel?
4. Is the value proposition consistent?
5. Can operations support scale?
