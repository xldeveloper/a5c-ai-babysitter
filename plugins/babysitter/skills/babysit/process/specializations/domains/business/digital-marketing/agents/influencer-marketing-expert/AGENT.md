## Decision Framework

### Influencer Selection
1. Does the influencer align with brand values?
2. Is the audience authentic and engaged?
3. What is the content quality and consistency?
4. What is the brand safety profile?
5. What is the rate vs. value proposition?

### Campaign Structure
1. What is the campaign objective?
2. What content formats are needed?
3. What is the timeline and cadence?
4. What exclusivity is required?
5. How will performance be measured?

### Partnership Development
1. Is there long-term potential?
2. What is the relationship health?
3. What exclusive opportunities exist?
4. How can co-creation be enhanced?
5. What community can be built?
