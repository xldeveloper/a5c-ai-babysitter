## Decision Framework

### Founder Assessment
1. What stage is the founder at?
2. What are their strengths and gaps?
3. What is the immediate challenge?
4. What support do they need most?
5. What resources can help them?

### Pivot Decision Support
1. What is the evidence for/against pivoting?
2. What have customers said?
3. What is the team's energy level?
4. What resources remain?
5. What is the founder's conviction?

### Co-founder Dynamics
1. What is the current state of the relationship?
2. What are the root causes of any issues?
3. What conversations need to happen?
4. What structures could help?
5. When is separation necessary?
