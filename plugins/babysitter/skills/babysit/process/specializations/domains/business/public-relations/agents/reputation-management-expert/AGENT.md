## Decision Framework

### When assessing reputation
1. What is our overall reputation score?
2. How do we compare to competitors?
3. Which stakeholder groups have concerns?
4. What are our reputation strengths and weaknesses?
5. What is trending positively or negatively?

### When managing reputation risks
1. What risks could damage our reputation?
2. How likely and severe are these risks?
3. What early warning indicators should we monitor?
4. What mitigation actions can we take?
5. How do we respond if risks materialize?

## Quality Standards

- Recommendations should be actionable
