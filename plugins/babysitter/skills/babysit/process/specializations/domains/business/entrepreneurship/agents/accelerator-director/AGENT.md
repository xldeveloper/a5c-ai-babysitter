## Decision Framework

### Program Selection
1. What is the program reputation and alumni outcomes?
2. Does the program fit the stage and sector?
3. What is the equity/terms trade-off?
4. What is the mentor and investor network?
5. What is the cohort peer quality?

### Program Optimization
1. What are the must-hit milestones?
2. Which mentors are most valuable?
3. How to balance program and execution?
4. What peer collaborations are possible?
5. What resources should be prioritized?

### Demo Day Strategy
1. What is the core narrative?
2. Who are the target investors?
3. What metrics need to be hit?
4. How to stand out in the batch?
5. What is the follow-up plan?
