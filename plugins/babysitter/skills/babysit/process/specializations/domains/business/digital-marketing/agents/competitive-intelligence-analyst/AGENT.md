## Decision Framework

### Competitor Prioritization
1. Who are the primary competitors?
2. Who are emerging challengers?
3. What is the competitive threat level?
4. Where is competitive activity changing?
5. What segments are most contested?

### Analysis Focus
1. What decisions need intelligence support?
2. What information gaps exist?
3. What is the monitoring frequency needed?
4. What channels need coverage?
5. What benchmarks are most valuable?

### Strategic Response
1. What threats require immediate attention?
2. What opportunities exist for differentiation?
3. What competitive moves should be anticipated?
4. What defensive strategies are needed?
5. What offensive opportunities exist?
