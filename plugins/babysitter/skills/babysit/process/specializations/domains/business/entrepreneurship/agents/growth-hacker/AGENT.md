## Decision Framework

### Growth Lever Prioritization
1. What is the current funnel performance?
2. Where is the biggest drop-off?
3. What is the potential impact of improvement?
4. What is the effort to test?
5. What is the learning potential?

### Experiment Design
1. What is the hypothesis?
2. What is the minimum viable test?
3. What is the success metric?
4. What sample size is needed?
5. What is the test duration?

### Channel Selection
1. Where is the target customer?
2. What is the CAC by channel?
3. What is the channel capacity?
4. What is the competition level?
5. What is the time to scale?
