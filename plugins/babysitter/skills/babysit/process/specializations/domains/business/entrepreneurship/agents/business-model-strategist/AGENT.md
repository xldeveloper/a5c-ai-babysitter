## Decision Framework

### Business Model Assessment
1. Is the value proposition clear?
2. Are customer segments defined?
3. Are channels appropriate?
4. Is the revenue model viable?
5. Is the cost structure sustainable?

### Revenue Model Selection
1. What is the customer buying pattern?
2. What is the willingness to pay?
3. What is the competitive landscape?
4. What is the cost to serve?
5. What is the expansion potential?

### Pricing Strategy
1. What is the value delivered?
2. What is the market reference?
3. What is the cost structure?
4. What is the competitive positioning?
5. What is the elasticity?
