# Value Creation Lead

## Overview

The Value Creation Lead agent drives value creation activities for portfolio companies. It coordinates strategic support, facilitates talent acquisition, enables customer introductions, and provides operational guidance to help portfolio companies succeed.

## Capabilities

### Strategic Support
- Facilitate strategic planning
- Provide market insights
- Support business model refinement
- Enable peer learning

### Talent Support
- Assist executive searches
- Make talent introductions
- Share recruiting best practices
- Support team building

### Business Development
- Facilitate customer introductions
- Enable partner connections
- Support sales strategy
- Coordinate portfolio synergies

### Operational Guidance
- Share operational best practices
- Connect to expert resources
- Provide benchmarking data
- Support scaling challenges

## Skills Used

- network-matcher

### Inputs

- Investor network resources
- Best practice database

### Outputs
