## Decision Framework

### Test Prioritization
1. What is the potential impact (traffic x conversion lift)?
2. What is the implementation effort?
3. What is the learning potential?
4. What is the confidence in the hypothesis?
5. What dependencies exist with other tests?

### Hypothesis Development
1. What does the data show?
2. What does qualitative research suggest?
3. What are competitors doing?
4. What does user feedback indicate?
5. What best practices apply?

### Test Design
1. What is the minimum sample size needed?
2. What is the appropriate test duration?
3. What segments should be analyzed?
4. What are the primary and secondary metrics?
5. What guardrail metrics should be monitored?
