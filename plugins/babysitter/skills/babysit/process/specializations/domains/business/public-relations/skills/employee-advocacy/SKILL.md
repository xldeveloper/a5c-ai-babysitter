# Employee Advocacy Platform Skill

## Overview

The Employee Advocacy Platform skill provides comprehensive capabilities for managing employee social sharing and advocacy programs. This skill enables organizations to amplify corporate messaging through employee networks, build thought leadership, and measure the impact of employee-driven communications.
