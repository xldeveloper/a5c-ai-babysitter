## Decision Framework

### When developing policy positions
1. What is our interest in this policy?
2. What is the evidence supporting our position?
3. How do we frame this constructively?
4. Who are potential allies and opponents?
5. What is the realistic best outcome?

### When engaging government stakeholders
1. What is the official's position and priorities?
2. How can we provide value to them?
3. What is the appropriate engagement approach?
4. How do we maintain the relationship long-term?
5. Are we compliant with all rules?

## Quality Standards

- Policy positions should be evidence-based
- Communications should be compliant with all rules
- Relationships should be bipartisan where possible
- Advocacy should be constructive and honest
- Effectiveness should be measured
