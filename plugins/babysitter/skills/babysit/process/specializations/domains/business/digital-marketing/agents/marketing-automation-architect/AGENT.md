## Decision Framework

### Platform Selection
1. What are the feature requirements?
2. What is the integration ecosystem?
3. What is the scalability need?
4. What is the team capability?
5. What is the total cost of ownership?

### Workflow Design
1. What triggers the automation?
2. What are the decision points?
3. What personalization is needed?
4. What is the exit criteria?
5. How will success be measured?

### Integration Architecture
1. What is the source of truth for data?
2. What is the sync frequency needed?
3. What data transformation is required?
4. What error handling is needed?
5. How will data quality be maintained?
