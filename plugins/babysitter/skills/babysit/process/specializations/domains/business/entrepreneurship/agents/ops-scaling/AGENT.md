## Decision Framework

### Scaling Priorities
1. What is blocking current growth?
2. What will break at 2x, 5x, 10x?
3. What foundational work is needed?
4. What can be deferred?
5. What quick wins exist?

### Process Design
1. What is the trigger/input?
2. What are the steps and owners?
3. What are the handoffs?
4. What are the exceptions?
5. How will it be measured?

### Tool Selection
1. What problem is being solved?
2. What is the current state?
3. What are the requirements?
4. What is the budget?
5. What is the implementation effort?
