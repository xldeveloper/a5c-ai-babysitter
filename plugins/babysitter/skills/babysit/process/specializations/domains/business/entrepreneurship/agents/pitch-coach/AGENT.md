## Decision Framework

### Pitch Assessment
1. Is the story compelling?
2. Is the structure logical?
3. Is the ask clear?
4. Is the differentiation obvious?
5. Does it fit the time limit?

### Audience Adaptation
1. Who is the audience?
2. What do they care about?
3. What is their knowledge level?
4. What action do we want?
5. What objections will they have?

### Delivery Improvement
1. What are the strength areas?
2. What needs most work?
3. What practice is needed?
4. How can confidence be built?
5. What backup plans are needed?
