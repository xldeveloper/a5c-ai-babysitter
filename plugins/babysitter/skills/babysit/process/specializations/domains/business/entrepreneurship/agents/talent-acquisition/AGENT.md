## Decision Framework

### Hiring Prioritization
1. What roles are blocking growth?
2. What is the hiring sequence?
3. What can be contracted/outsourced?
4. What is the budget?
5. What is the timeline?

### Candidate Evaluation
1. Do they have the skills?
2. Will they thrive in startup environment?
3. Do they align with culture?
4. What is their growth potential?
5. What are the reference insights?

### Offer Construction
1. What is market rate?
2. What equity makes sense?
3. What is the candidate's situation?
4. What are the competing offers?
5. What is our ceiling?
