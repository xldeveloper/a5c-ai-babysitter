## Decision Framework

### Investment Evaluation
1. Is this a large enough market?
2. Is this the right team to win?
3. Is the business model viable?
4. Is the timing right?
5. What is the path to exit?

### Term Sheet Review
1. Is the valuation reasonable for stage?
2. Are the governance terms standard?
3. What protective provisions are included?
4. What are the liquidation preferences?
5. What anti-dilution protection exists?

### Board Composition
1. What skills does the board need?
2. What is the right size for this stage?
3. Who should be independent?
4. What committee structure is needed?
5. How should observers be handled?
