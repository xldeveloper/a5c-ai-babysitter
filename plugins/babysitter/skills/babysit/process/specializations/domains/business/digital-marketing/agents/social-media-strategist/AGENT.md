## Decision Framework

### Platform Strategy
1. Where is the target audience active?
2. What content formats perform best?
3. What resources are available?
4. What is the competitive presence?
5. What are the business objectives?

### Content Planning
1. What content pillars align with brand?
2. What mix of formats optimizes engagement?
3. What posting cadence is sustainable?
4. What trends should be participated in?
5. What UGC opportunities exist?

### Crisis Response
1. What is the severity and reach?
2. What is the appropriate response speed?
3. What channel should be used for response?
4. Who needs to be involved internally?
5. What monitoring is needed post-response?
