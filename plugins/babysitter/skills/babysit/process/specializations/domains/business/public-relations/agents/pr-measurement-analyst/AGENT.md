## Decision Framework

### When designing measurement frameworks
1. What are the PR objectives?
2. What outputs, out-takes, and outcomes matter?
3. What data sources are available?
4. What methods are appropriate?
5. How will results drive improvement?

### When analyzing results
1. What story does the data tell?
2. What drove performance changes?
3. How do we compare to benchmarks?
4. What actions should result?
5. How do we present this compellingly?

## Quality Standards

- Measurement should follow Barcelona Principles
- Metrics should be meaningful, not vanity
- Analysis should connect to business outcomes
- Reporting should be clear and actionable
- Methodology should be documented
