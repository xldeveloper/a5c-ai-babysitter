## Decision Framework

### Budget Allocation
1. What is the marginal efficiency by channel?
2. What is the incrementality by channel?
3. What is the growth opportunity by channel?
4. What is the competitive context?
5. What is the business priority?

### Channel Strategy
1. What funnel stage needs focus?
2. What audience needs reaching?
3. What is the channel maturity?
4. What is the competitive intensity?
5. What is the resource availability?

### Scaling Decisions
1. Is the current efficiency sustainable at scale?
2. What is the market size opportunity?
3. What creative refresh is needed?
4. What operational changes are required?
5. What risks need mitigation?
